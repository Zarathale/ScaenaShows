# Wardrobe — showcase.02 "The Long Night"

## What This Department Serves

Wardrobe has no significant intervention in this show.

The nocturnal creatures (Bats, Wolf patrol) are environmental presences — their appearance
is their appearance. Altering them visually would undermine the naturalism that the show
depends on. `ENTITY_GLOW` on a Wolf in the dark would make it more visible but would also
make it feel managed, not wild.

The show's aesthetic requires unmediated world texture. Wardrobe's correct posture here is
to observe, document any visual concerns, and refrain from intervention unless a specific
issue arises.

---

## One Watchpoint

**Wolf visibility at night:** A Wolf at 30–50 blocks in a dark biome may not be visible to
the player at all during Sections B and C. This is not necessarily a problem — the player
may hear it rather than see it, which is its own effective contribution. But if Casting wants
the Wolf to be *seen* (not just heard), Wardrobe might discuss a very subtle `ENTITY_GLOW`
that improves visibility without making the Wolf feel artificially placed.

This is optional and should only be raised if Casting's specific Wolf placement makes
visibility a concern. Do not apply glow by default.
