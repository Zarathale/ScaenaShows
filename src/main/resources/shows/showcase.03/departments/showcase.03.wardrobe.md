# Wardrobe — showcase.03 "Welcome"

## What This Department Serves

Wardrobe has one specific question to answer for this show: does the welcoming committee
have a visual identity as a committee?

The show's premise is a welcome — a gathering of residents who are glad to have you. If those
residents look random and disparate, they read as a zoo. If they share something visual —
even one subtle quality — they read as a community.

This is a smaller-stakes version of showcase.01's collection visual language decision. The
same question; a different context. There, the Allay was a collector and its companions were
a collection. Here, the committee members are residents of the same world, gathered for the
same purpose.

---

## The Committee Visual Language

**The question Wardrobe answers:** Is there one visual quality shared across the welcoming
committee that makes them feel like they belong to the same moment?

**Option A — No imposed visual logic.** The committee reads as diverse and genuine — different
species, different appearances, brought together by circumstance. The visual coherence comes
from their shared facing and positioning, not from any Wardrobe intervention. Valid if the
species mix is well-chosen.

**Option B — A shared glow.** `ENTITY_GLOW` applied to all committee members in the same
color — a warm gold, a soft white. In the warm Build lighting, the committee has a subtle
luminance that separates them from the background and gives them visual coherence. This is
the most legible Wardrobe tool and the most accessible.

**Option C — Species-informed coherence.** If Casting has chosen species with similar visual
qualities (e.g., all small entities, or all entities with ambient luminance like Allays), no
Wardrobe intervention is needed — the species choices already create coherence. Wardrobe
notes this and takes no action.

Wardrobe reviews Casting's committee selection at intake and makes a recommendation. The
committee must be confirmed before Wardrobe can advise.

---

## Notes on this Show's Context

This show may become actual onboarding for new ScaenaCraft players. The visual impression
of the welcoming committee is the first impression of the server's community. Wardrobe's
decision here has real-world consequence. Design as if you're setting a standard.

A glowing committee in a warm color (gold or white) on a well-built Welcome Stage is a
strong, legible, welcoming image. It is also modular — if the committee changes for future
uses, the glow behavior can be applied to new entities without redesigning the scene.

If `ENTITY_GLOW` is used: confirm the glow color works in Lighting's Build and Celebration
TIME_OF_DAY conditions. A gold glow in warm evening light looks like it belongs; in bright
daylight it may be less distinct. Coordinate with Lighting.

## Intake Questions for Wardrobe

1. **Committee visual language:** After seeing Casting's committee selection — Option A
   (no intervention), B (shared glow), or C (species coherence sufficient)?
2. **Glow color (if Option B):** What color? In what Lighting conditions does this read best?
3. **Glow lifetime:** Applied at committee staging (before player arrives) and maintained
   through the show? Confirm with Choreography/Casting on entity persistence.
