## Line Notes

**Highest-ambiguity lines** (recontextualize at reveal):
- B.2: "you move too fast for heavy" — describes the Vindicator; player is present
- D.2: "I want you moving — right until the end" — right until the end of *what*
- E.2: "I know what you walk toward" — the Vindicator walks toward battle
- F.1: "I kept coming back to this place. It answers something." — the show's most
  loaded single line; final expedition opener; the Armorer chose this place for the weapon

**Departure pattern** — the Armorer is always ahead:
"This way. I'll already be there." → "Keep up. I'll be there." →
"Meet me there." → "I'll go ahead. Take your time."
Each return gives the player slightly more autonomy. By A4, the Armorer has stopped
instructing. The player knows the way.

**"You've been good company"** — A-Final line 2 is the first moment in the show
explicitly addressed to the player. Every prior "you" was the Vindicator.
