# Casting Director — Archetype Sampler

## Brief Received

> No performers in this show. Entity system is not used.

---

## Decisions

**Decision: No cast.** This show is entirely environmental and player-focused. The player is the only entity in the space. The show's emotional arc is achieved through atmosphere, movement, sound, and voice — not through the presence of other beings. A performer would compete with the intimacy the show is trying to build.

---

## Notes

R1–R7: No changes to casting. The absence of performers has been a consistent and deliberate choice across all revisions.
